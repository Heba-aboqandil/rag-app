Ed Sheeran has released several successful albums:

1. "+" (Plus) - Released in 2011. This album made him famous and includes emotional songs like "The A Team".

2. "x" (Multiply) - Released in 2014. Includes popular songs like "Thinking Out Loud" and "Photograph".

3. "÷" (Divide) - Released in 2017. One of his most successful albums, featuring "Shape of You" and "Perfect".

4. "=" (Equals) - Released in 2021. Focuses on personal life and emotions.

Each album shows his growth as an artist and explores different musical styles.
