Ed Sheeran is an English singer-songwriter born on February 17, 1991, in Halifax, England.

He started playing guitar at a young age and began writing songs as a teenager. Ed moved to London to pursue his music career and performed in many small venues.

His breakthrough came in 2011 with the release of his debut album "+" (Plus), which included hit songs like "The A Team".

Ed Sheeran is known for his unique style that combines pop, folk, and acoustic music. He is also famous for writing his own songs and performing live using a loop pedal.

Today, he is one of the most successful artists in the world.
